Design notes. Per-room accent colours are used sparingly — as a small icon tint and a faint halo behind the tile icon, not as a full background wash. Active lights get a soft amber glow plus a warmer card background so they feel lit. Unavailable devices are never hidden; they get a quiet red tint. The "last seen" subtitle is a placeholder that in the real UI will be derived from last_updated. Room tile counts ("8 lights") are similarly derived and could be dropped if you want the home screen sparser.
